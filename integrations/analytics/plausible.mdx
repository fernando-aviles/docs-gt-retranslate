---
title: "Plausible"
---

Configure Plausible analytics by adding your site's domain to `mint.json`:

<CodeGroup>

```json mint.json
"analytics": {
    "plausible": {
        "domain": "docs.domain.com"
    }
}
```

</CodeGroup>

<Note>
Do not include `http://` or `https://` in the domain.
</Note>
