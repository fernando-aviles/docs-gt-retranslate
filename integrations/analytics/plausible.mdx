---
title: "Plausible"
---

To enable Plausible analytics, add your site's domain to `mint.json`:

<CodeGroup>

```json mint.json
"analytics": {
    "plausible": {
        "domain": "docs.domain.com"
    }
}
```

</CodeGroup>

<Info>
    Do not include `http://` or `https://` in your domain.
</Info>
</fileContent>
