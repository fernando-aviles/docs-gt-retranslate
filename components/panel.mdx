---
title: 'Panel'
description: 'Specify the content of the right side panel'
icon: 'panel-right'
---

You can use the `<Panel>` widget to customize the right side panel of a page with any widgets that you want.

If a page has a `<Panel>` widget, any [RequestExample](/widgets/examples#request-example) and [ResponseExample](/widgets/examples#response-example) widgets must be inside `<Panel>`.

The widgets in a `<Panel>` will replace a page's table of contents.

````mdx
<Panel>
  <Info>Pin info to the side panel. Or add any other component.</Info>
</Panel>
````
