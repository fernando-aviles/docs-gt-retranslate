Our callout components help emphasize important information in your content. Here are the available callout types:

<Note>Use Notes for general informational content</Note>
<Warning>Use Warnings for cautionary messages or potential issues</Warning>
<Info>Use Info for additional context and detailed information</Info>
<Tip>Use Tips for helpful suggestions and best practices</Tip>
<Check>Use Check for success messages or completion status</Check>

### Usage

All callouts follow the same syntax:
```jsx
<ComponentName>Your message here</ComponentName>
```

Replace `ComponentName` with any of: `Note`, `Warning`, `Info`, `Tip`, or `Check`.
