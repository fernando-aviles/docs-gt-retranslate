The `CardGroup` component lets you group multiple `Card` components together in a grid layout.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="First Card" icon="square-1">
    Example card content
  </Card>
  <Card title="Second Card" icon="square-2">
    Another card example
  </Card>
  <Card title="Third Card" icon="square-3">
    Third sample card
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fourth Card" icon="square-4">
    Fourth sample card
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
