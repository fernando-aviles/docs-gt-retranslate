Browser cache can sometimes prevent you from seeing the latest version of your documentation. If your changes aren't appearing after publishing, clearing your browser cache often resolves the issue.

## Chrome

### Quick method
Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>Delete</kbd> (Windows/Linux) or <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>Delete</kbd> (Mac) to open the Clear browsing data dialog.

### Manual method
1. Click the three dots menu in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Click **Privacy and security** in the left sidebar
4. Select **Clear browsing data**
5. Choose **Advanced** tab
6. Select time range (recommend "All time" for complete cache clearing)
7. Check **Cached images and files**
8. Click **Clear data**

### Hard refresh
For a quick cache refresh of the current page, press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>F5</kbd> (Windows/Linux) or <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>R</kbd> (Mac).

## Safari

### Quick method
Press <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Option</kbd> + <kbd>E</kbd> to empty caches immediately.

### Manual method
1. Click **Safari** in the menu bar
2. Select **Settings** (or **Preferences** in older versions)
3. Go to the **Advanced** tab
4. Check **Show Develop menu in menu bar**
5. Click **Develop** in the menu bar
6. Select **Empty Caches**

### Alternative method
1. Click **Safari** in the menu bar
2. Select **Clear History...**
3. Choose time range from dropdown
4. Click **Clear History**

### Hard refresh
Press <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>R</kbd> to reload the page and bypass cache.

## Firefox

### Quick method
Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>Delete</kbd> (Windows/Linux) or <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>Delete</kbd> (Mac).

### Manual method
1. Click the hamburger menu (three lines) in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Click **Privacy & Security** in the left sidebar
4. Scroll to **Cookies and Site Data** section
5. Click **Clear Data**
6. Check **Cached Web Content**
7. Click **Clear**

### Hard refresh
Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>F5</kbd> (Windows/Linux) or <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>R</kbd> (Mac).

## Microsoft Edge

### Quick method
Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>Delete</kbd> to open the clear browsing data dialog.

### Manual method
1. Click the three dots menu in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Click **Privacy, search, and services** in the left sidebar
4. Under **Clear browsing data**, click **Choose what to clear**
5. Select time range
6. Check **Cached images and files**
7. Click **Clear now**

### Hard refresh
Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>F5</kbd> to reload the page and bypass cache.

## Opera

### Manual method
1. Click the **Opera** menu in the top-left corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Click **Advanced** in the left sidebar, then **Privacy & security**
4. Click **Clear browsing data**
5. Select **Advanced** tab
6. Choose time range
7. Check **Cached images and files**
8. Click **Clear data**

### Hard refresh
Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>F5</kbd> (Windows/Linux) or <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>R</kbd> (Mac).

## Mobile browsers

### Chrome (Android)
1. Open Chrome app
2. Tap the three dots menu
3. Select **Settings**
4. Tap **Privacy and security**
5. Select **Clear browsing data**
6. Choose **Advanced** tab
7. Select time range
8. Check **Cached images and files**
9. Tap **Clear data**

### Safari (iOS)
1. Open **Settings** app
2. Scroll down and tap **Safari**
3. Tap **Clear History and Website Data**
4. Confirm by tapping **Clear History and Data**

<Warning>
This will clear all Safari browsing history and data, not just cache.
</Warning>

### Firefox (Mobile)
1. Open Firefox app
2. Tap the three dots menu
3. Select **Settings**
4. Tap **Data Management**
5. Select **Cache**
6. Tap **Clear Cache**

## When to clear cache

Clear your browser cache when:

- Documentation changes aren't appearing after publishing
- You see outdated content or styling
- Pages are loading incorrectly or showing errors
- You're troubleshooting display issues

<Tip>
If you frequently need to see the latest changes during development, consider using your browser's developer tools to disable cache while DevTools is open.
</Tip>

## Alternative solutions

If clearing cache doesn't resolve the issue:

1. Try opening the page in an incognito/private browsing window
2. Check if the issue occurs in a different browser
3. Verify your documentation has finished deploying
4. Contact support if problems persist

For deployment-related issues, see our [troubleshooting guide](/editor/troubleshooting) or [contact support](/contact-support).
